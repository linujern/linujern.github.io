Burglar week 16

Fifth week of game project 2

  • Back to Week Overview

Monday, April 17th

During day one of week five, we attended lectures held by Johno of the game studio Nornware, and by Science Center 2047. During the first lecture, which I found particularly informative and interesting, we discussed game development, game design and programming with the experienced indie dev Johno.

Tuesday, April 18th

Tuesday, I had plans to work on a save system for the player's settings and progress. Instead, I had an idea to simplify the UI design and started working on that. I also spent time helping Felicia on the artist side in creating the assets I needed for the notepad/clipboard.

In the afternoon, the whole school listened to a lecture by Moritz Hammer on behalf of Embark studios.

Above, kitchenware by Ellinor, a grand clock by Wille, speed props by Felicia and level work by Lucas.

Wednesday, April 19th

Another day filled with attending lectures.

Thursday, April 20th

Thursday I quickly set up all the sound classes we will need for the game, and got put in the logic which allows our player to interact with the clipboard UI that I am implementing. I also found an issue in the logic I had written for the UI system, so I sat down to plan some new solutions. In the afternoon, a daughter company of Zordix came and held a lecture for the school, which everyone attended. I also managed to replace many of my temporary clipboard assets with the actual models.

Above, you see a populated upstairs living room area, a bed by Loke, texturing progress on the antagonist by Oskar, and vegetation by Felicia.

Friday, April 21st

During the last day of week five, I worked on a save system. I also created a selection screen where the player can choose which save file to play on. Then, I worked some more on fixing the logic of the clipboard, before Unreal Engine crashed and corrupted the files I had worked on that day. Being unable to recover them, I got to work redoing all the work I had lost.

Valuables which the player must steal, by Felicia. A lamp, by Wille, and kitchen assets by Ellinor.
